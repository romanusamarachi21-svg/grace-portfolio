HEALTHCARE ANALYTICS PROJECT

Blood Bank Inventory & Wastage Analytics

Analyzing blood supply, utilization patterns, expiry trends, and wastage to improve inventory decisions.

Project Overview

Blood banks must maintain a balance between having enough blood available for patients while minimizing expiry and wastage. This project analyzes inventory performance and identifies patterns affecting blood availability and resource utilization.

Objective

  • Analyze blood inventory levels
  • Identify wastage and expiry trends
  • Understand demand patterns by blood group
  • Support better inventory management decisions
